Baked Mac & Cheese

Description

This cheesy and creamy side dish will just melt in your mouth.

So simple yet so good that you can make this any time of year. From holidays to birthday's everyone will request that you bring the Mac!

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salt
  • Olive oil (or any cooking oil of your choice)
  • Block of cheese (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup Whole milk
  • 1 Egg

    • Steps

    1. Step 1. Bring pot of water to a boil. Place macaroni into pot and add a good amount of salt for taste and oil to prevent sticking. When its al dente place in a strainer and rinse with cold water.
    2. Step 2. Prepare cheese. If you are also using a block of cheese cut the cheese into slices. Place half of cooled macaroni into pan.
    3. Step 3. Place shredded macaroni on top and repeat. If using block of cheese place evenly on top. Make sure everything is even.
    4. Step 4. In a bowl quickly mix mil and egg and pour all over macaroni evenly.
    5. Step 5. Place in oven c=and cook until the top is golden or until all the cheese has melted. The enjoy!
